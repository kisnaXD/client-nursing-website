Exclusive Partnership with Artemis Hospitals

Redefining Global Healthcare Mobility

At Ed2Careers, we’re proud to be the exclusive partner on the Global Workforce Mobility agenda with Artemis Hospitals. Together, we are reshaping healthcare training and bridging the gap between skilled Indian healthcare professionals and exciting opportunities in the UK healthcare sector.

Partnering For Excellence

Artemis Hospitals, a JCI-accredited, multi-specialty healthcare facility in Gurugram (Gurgaon), Delhi, India, is a trusted name in delivering exceptional care. With 700 beds, state-of-the-art infrastructure, cutting-edge technology, and a dedicated team of medical professionals, Artemis stands as a beacon of quality healthcare for both national and international patients.

Partnership that Stands Out

State-of-the-Art Facilities

Artemis provides world-class medical equipment and technology, enabling healthcare professionals to train in a realistic, high-tech environment and ensuring they are prepared for global challenges.

Credibility & Expertise

As a renowned and accredited partner, we combine clinical excellence with global training standards and soft skills, English language, and personality development partners for Artemis Hospitals.

Proven & Ethical Practices

With Artemis, we have successfully placed 200 caregivers in the UK in our very first year by adhering to strict ethical recruitment standards and ensuring compliance with UK immigration and Care Quality Commission guidelines.
